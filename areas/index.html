Areas We Cover

Sussex, Kent, Surrey,
London — and beyond.

Our showroom is in Hailsham, East Sussex. We install throughout Sussex, Kent, Surrey and London as standard. We take on projects anywhere in the UK and internationally for the right brief.

Our standard coverage area.

We visit homes, hotels, schools and commercial buildings throughout the following areas with no travel surcharge:

East & West Sussex

Including Hailsham, Eastbourne, Brighton, Hove, Lewes, Hastings, Bexhill, Worthing, Chichester, Horsham and all surrounding towns and villages. This is our home territory — we know it well.

Kent

Tunbridge Wells, Sevenoaks, Tonbridge, Maidstone, Canterbury, Folkestone, Ashford, Rye and the surrounding area. Many of our school and hotel projects are in Kent.

Surrey

Guildford, Reigate, Dorking, Epsom, Farnham and the wider county. Surrey is the area where we do the most large-scale residential work — manor houses, country estates and architect-designed new builds.

London

Throughout Greater London and the prime central London postcodes (PCL). We work with interior designers and architects across London regularly, particularly on high-end residential and boutique hospitality projects.

Beyond our standard area.

We take on projects outside our standard coverage area where the project is the right fit. We have installed across the UK — from Scotland to Cornwall — and internationally. If you have a project that requires our expertise, please get in touch regardless of location.

For external solar shading and MicroLouvre® projects in particular, we are one of only seven UK authorised installers, which means clients sometimes need to come to us regardless of geography. We will always discuss national or international projects on their merits.

Not in our coverage area?

Tell us about your project. If we can help, we will tell you honestly. If we cannot, we will tell you that too.
