OUR BLIND RANGE

Every blind type. Every window.

We supply and install all major blind types to measure. We advise on which works best for your window, light requirement and interior — including specialist solutions for shaped windows and skylights.

Most Popular

Roller Blinds

Clean-lined and versatile. Available in blackout, dim-out, screen and sheer. Manual spring or motorised. Side channels available for true blackout performance.

Roman Blinds

Soft fabric blinds that fold into neat horizontal pleats when raised. Made in any fabric to match your curtains. Battery or mains motorisation available.

Best Thermal

Duette & Pleated

Honeycomb fabric cells trap air for exceptional thermal and acoustic performance. Available top-down/bottom-up. Ideal for conservatories and problem glazing.

Venetian Blinds

Aluminium or timber slats with adjustable tilt for precise light control. Slim 16mm through to 50mm timber. Perfect for offices, studies and kitchens.

Statement

Silhouette Blinds

Hunter Douglas's signature blind — horizontal fabric vanes suspended between sheer fabric panels for a beautiful, architectural effect. Light control without sacrificing view.

Vertical Blinds

Fabric or PVC louvres for wide or patio windows. Stacked neatly to one or both sides. Available in a wide range of fabrics including dim-out and blackout.

Specialist

Shaped Windows & Skylights

Triangular gables, arched heads, roof lanterns, Velux skylights, flat rooflights and bay windows. Every solution made to template. Motorisation usually recommended for overhead glazing.

TRUE BLACKOUT

Blackout that actually works.

Most blackout blinds aren't. Light leaks down the sides, across the bottom, around every gap. We specify cassette systems with side channels and bottom rails that eliminate edge bleed. EN 13120 Class 3 — the only classification that matters.

Read the Blackout Guide

MOTORISATION

Control at your fingertips.

All blind types can be motorised. Somfy, Lutron and Velux compatible. Mains, rechargeable battery and solar-powered options. Works with Apple HomeKit, Google Home, Amazon Alexa and most building management systems.

Ideal for large or high windows, grouped rooms, and anywhere you want automation — wake-up scenes, scheduled privacy, solar-triggered closing.
