Why shutters

Light control, privacy and permanence.

Shutters adjust without needing to be opened or closed — the louvre tilt lets you dial in exactly the light level and privacy you want at any time of day. They look equally good open or closed.

They don't fade, sag, or collect dust the way fabric does. They're easy to wipe clean. And because they're fitted to the reveal, they frame the window perfectly — every window, every shape.

Configuration options

The right configuration for every window.

Full-height shutters

Full-Height

A single panel or pair covering the full window height. The cleanest, most architectural look. Works with any louvre size.

Tier-on-tier shutters

Tier-on-Tier

Upper and lower panels that open independently. Full privacy below, open above — or vice versa. Classic for sash windows and period properties.

Café-style shutters

Café-Style

Covering the lower half only. Maximum light through the upper pane, complete privacy at street level. Perfect for ground-floor front rooms.

Solid panel shutters

Solid Panel

No louvres — a solid painted panel. Period-accurate for Georgian and Regency properties. Excellent blackout performance and the most traditional aesthetic.

Louvre sizes

The louvre size changes the whole look.

Smaller louvres are more traditional; larger louvres are contemporary and let in more light when open. We advise on the right size for your window proportions and interior style.
