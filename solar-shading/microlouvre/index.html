What Is It?

Thinner than anything else available.

MicroLouvre® is a UK-manufactured external solar screen comprising ultra-fine aluminium louvre blades arranged at a fixed angle. At any given screen thickness, it achieves a lower g-value than any comparable product on the market.

Unlike conventional roller screens, MicroLouvre® is a rigid system — it does not roll or retract. This makes it especially suitable for heritage properties where motorised cassettes would be inappropriate, and for commercial applications where robustness and longevity are required.

The fine blade pitch creates a visual effect that is remarkably discreet from the interior — the outside world remains clearly visible while solar gain is dramatically reduced.

Applications

Where MicroLouvre® works best.

Listed Buildings & Conservation Areas

The slim blade profile is more sympathetic to heritage settings than conventional roller screens or awnings. We have successfully achieved planning approval for MicroLouvre® installations in conservation areas in East Sussex and Surrey.

Premium Residential

Where the visual impact of the shading system matters as much as its performance. MicroLouvre® is near-invisible from inside — the blade geometry allows clear outward views while blocking the majority of direct solar radiation.

Commercial & Educational

Fixed panel systems suit curtain-wall and structural glazing applications. Schools, offices and healthcare facilities benefit from the low maintenance requirements — no moving parts, no motorisation required unless preferred.
