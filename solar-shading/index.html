Why external?

Internal blinds intercept heat after it has entered the building. External shading stops it before it gets through the glass.

A well-specified external roller screen with a g-value of 0.12 reduces solar heat gain by up to 88%. The same window with an internal blind: less than 15%. The physics are unambiguous. That is why Part O and TM59 both require external solutions for compliance.
