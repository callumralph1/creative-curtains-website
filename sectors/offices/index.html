Discuss Your Office
The Challenge

Glare makes screens unusable.
Heat makes people unproductive.

South and west-facing offices present a recurring challenge: direct sun makes screens unreadable and raises temperatures beyond the comfort zone. The correct answer is solar-control fabric — a roller blind that reduces solar gain and glare while maintaining diffused daylight. For more challenging facades, external shading removes solar energy before it reaches the glass entirely.

BREEAM & EPC

Creditable toward your building assessment.

External solar shading contributes to BREEAM Hea 04 and Ene credits. We supply full compliance documentation for your assessor. For EPC improvement, reducing solar gain through external shading reduces peak cooling load and can move a building up an EPC band. We provide technical data in the format your assessor needs.
