HTM Compliance

Window treatments for
clinical environments.

We supply and install window treatments for NHS Trusts, private hospitals, GP surgeries, care homes and specialist clinical facilities. All products are specified in line with HTM (Health Technical Memoranda) requirements and can be provided with full compliance documentation.

Wipe-clean fabrics are standard for clinical areas. We also supply and install anti-bacterial coated roller blinds for treatment rooms, wards and procedure areas. For areas where infection control is paramount, blindless solutions such as integral blinds within double-glazed units can be discussed.

External solar shading for healthcare buildings reduces ward overheating — a patient safety issue as well as a comfort one — and contributes to BREEAM and NHS Net Zero targets.
