Hotels & Hospitality

Window treatments
for hotels, restaurants
and spa facilities.

Bedroom blackout. Restaurant ambience. Spa privacy. External terrace shading. Contract-grade product, motorisation as standard, and installation scheduled around your occupancy. We understand that a closed bedroom is a lost night's revenue.

Discuss Your Project
Applications

Every room in the building.

Bedrooms

Complete blackout is non-negotiable for guest satisfaction. We install motorised blackout roller blinds or blackout curtains with wave or pencil pleat heading. Two-year guarantee on all motors. We work floor-by-floor to minimise room downtime.

Restaurants & Bars

Linen roller blinds and Roman blinds for light diffusion and ambience control. We specify fabric that wipes clean, does not fade under commercial lighting, and holds its appearance under daily use. FR-rated as standard.

Spa & Wellness

Moisture-resistant blinds for treatment rooms and pool areas. Automated dimming for ambience. External solar shading for overheating control in glazed spa facilities — particularly important for south and west-facing pool enclosures.

Terraces & Outdoor Dining

Motorised louvre pergolas extend your outdoor dining season. Awnings protect al fresco areas from direct sun. External roller screens provide privacy screening for terrace areas. We install and maintain.

Conference & Events

Motorised blackout for AV presentations. Full integration with room control systems via Somfy or Crestron. One-touch scene control for different meeting configurations. We programme and test before handover.

Lobbies & Public Areas

Statement curtains and large-format sheers for reception areas. We have experience with floor-to-double-height-ceiling installations. All fabric specified for durability and cleanability in high-footfall environments.

How We Work with Hotels

Installation around
your occupancy.

We understand the constraints of working in a live hotel. We schedule installation floor-by-floor or wing-by-wing. We work evenings and weekends where occupancy requires it. We do not leave a bedroom out of service overnight.

All our operatives are trained to hotel standards — they are respectful of guest areas, they remove debris as they go, and they communicate directly with your facilities team throughout.
