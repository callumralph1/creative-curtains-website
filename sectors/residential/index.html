Residential

Every window treatment
for your home.

Curtains, blinds, shutters and external solar shading for homes in Sussex, Kent, Surrey and London. We visit your home, measure precisely, guide you through fabric and product selection, and install everything ourselves.

Book a Home Visit
Our Approach

We visit first. Everything flows from there.

We visit your property at a time that suits you. We measure every window, look at how the light moves through each room throughout the day, and discuss what you are trying to achieve — privacy, blackout, solar control, decoration, or all of them — and recommend accordingly.

We produce a written quotation with no obligation to proceed. When you do decide to go ahead, we handle everything: manufacture, delivery and installation.

What We Install

The full range.

Curtains

Made-to-measure in linen, wool, velvet and silk. Every heading style. Interlined, blackout and sheer options.

Blinds

Roman, roller, Venetian, cellular and motorised. Blackout for bedrooms, solar-control for south-facing rooms.

Shutters

Solid timber or MDF, painted to any RAL or Farrow & Ball colour. Full-height, cafe-style and bay window systems.

 Part O Compliant

External Solar Shading

Roller screens, louvre systems, awnings and pergolas. The only product that satisfies Part O overheating regulations.
