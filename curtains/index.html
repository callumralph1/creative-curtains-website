Heading styles

The heading defines the look.

From relaxed and contemporary to formal and deeply pleated — we advise on the right choice for your fabric weight, window height and interior style.

Triple pleat heading

Triple / Pinch Pleat

The classic choice. Three evenly spaced pleats create a structured, formal drape. Works beautifully in heavier fabrics and for tall drops.

Eyelet heading

Eyelet / Ring Top

Contemporary and relaxed. Metal rings thread directly onto the pole for a casual, clean ripple. Best with mid-weight fabrics.

Wave heading

Wave / S-Fold

A continuous, even wave that reads as beautifully architectural. Requires a specialist track and looks superb in sheer and semi-sheer fabrics.

Goblet pleat

Goblet Pleat

A formal, elegant heading where the pleat is shaped into a cup. Often seen in period properties and luxury hotel bedrooms.

Pencil pleat

Pencil Pleat

Dense, tightly gathered pleats create a full, traditional look. Versatile and works with most fabric weights and window sizes.

Tab top heading

Tab Top

Fabric loops thread directly over a pole. Relaxed and informal. Works best in lighter weight fabrics and casual living spaces.

Lining options

The right lining changes everything.

Lining adds body, improves drape, extends fabric life and controls light and heat. We'll advise on the right choice for each room.

Standard Lining

Cotton sateen lining. Adds body and protects the face fabric. The baseline for all curtains.

Blackout Lining

Acrylic-coated to block light. EN 13120 Class 3 with proper edge sealing. Essential for bedrooms.

Interlined

A layer of bump between face fabric and lining. Creates luxuriously full, heavy drape. Improves thermal and acoustic performance.

Thermal Lining

Silvered lining reflects heat back into the room in winter and blocks solar gain in summer. Good for period properties.

Smart living

Motorised curtains, quietly done.

Wall switch, remote, app or voice. Somfy tubular motors integrate with Apple HomeKit, Google Home and any BMS. Invisible in the track. Whisper quiet. We specify and install.
