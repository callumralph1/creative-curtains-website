Privacy Policy.

How Creative Curtains and Blinds collects, uses and protects your personal data. Last updated June 2026.

Who we are

Creative Curtains and Blinds, 9 North Street, Hailsham, East Sussex, BN27 1DQ. You can contact us at callum@creativecurtains.uk.com or on 01323 844555.

What data we collect

When you submit an enquiry via our website, we collect your name, email address, telephone number and the details of your enquiry. We do not collect any other personal data through our website.

We use a cookie to remember that you have accepted this privacy notice (a technical cookie). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies or analytics cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or any similar tracking service.

How we use your data

We use your contact details solely to respond to your enquiry and to provide the services you have requested. We do not send marketing emails unless you have specifically requested them. We do not share your data with any third party.

How long we keep it

We retain enquiry records for six years in accordance with our contractual and tax obligations, after which they are securely deleted. If you have not become a customer, we delete your enquiry data within 12 months of last contact.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access the data we hold about you, to correct it, to request its deletion, and to object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at callum@creativecurtains.uk.com.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

Netlify Forms

Our contact form is processed by Netlify, Inc. Form submissions are transmitted securely. Netlify's privacy policy is available at netlify.com/privacy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at this URL.
