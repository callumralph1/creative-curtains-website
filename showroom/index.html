Showroom

Hailsham Showroom,
East Sussex.

9 North Street, Hailsham, BN27 1DQ. Fabric samples, working motorisation demonstrations and the full external shading range on display. Open Monday to Friday 9–5:30, Saturday 9–4.

What You Will Find

See it, touch it,
hear it work.

Our showroom carries fabric samples from all the major suppliers we work with — linen, wool, cotton, velvet and blackout fabrics in hundreds of colourways. We have working blind and curtain demonstrations and a full external shading display.

If you are interested in motorisation, we can demonstrate the full Somfy range including smart home integration. You can hear how quiet the motors are, see the different operating speeds and try the app controls.

For external solar shading, we have product samples and can show you the difference in solar heat gain between different fabric openness factors and g-values.
